Accreditation

By checking this box you represent that you qualify as an "accredited investor" under SEC Rule 501. This means you either:

  • have a net worth in excess of $1,000,000, not including your primary residence
  • you (or you and your spouse) have previously made, in each of the prior two years, and expect to make this calendar year, more than $200,000 ($300,000 if with the spouse)
  • you are an entity meeting another test under the rule.
